# Business Recommendations

## Key Findings

- Moderate activity users represented the largest customer segment.
- Highly active users consistently recorded more steps and calories burned.
- Some users showed low engagement, presenting opportunities for increased product usage.

## Recommendations

### 1. Personalized Marketing

Develop targeted marketing campaigns for each activity segment using personalized goals, reminders, and rewards.

### 2. Increase User Engagement

Encourage low-activity users through daily challenges, milestone badges, and motivational notifications.

### 3. Promote Premium Features

Offer premium subscriptions or advanced wellness insights to highly active users who demonstrate consistent engagement.

### 4. Improve Customer Retention

Use activity trends to send personalized wellness recommendations and celebrate user achievements to increase long-term retention.

## Expected Business Impact

- Increased customer engagement
- Higher app usage
- Improved customer retention
- Stronger customer loyalty
- Better data-driven marketing decisions
